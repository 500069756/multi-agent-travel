## Practical Notes

- Book tickets in advance for popular attractions like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum.
- Consider purchasing a Paris Museum Pass for skip-the-line access and cost savings.
- Explore Le Marais on foot to discover its charming atmosphere and historic landmarks.
- Don't forget to try a classic French croissant at a local bakery like Le Petit Grain or Boulangerie Miettes.
- Be prepared for crowds and lines at popular attractions, and plan accordingly.
- Learn basic French phrases like 'bonjour' and 'merci' to enhance your interactions with locals.
